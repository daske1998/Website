Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the club?

To join the Procrastination Club, simply click on the 'Join the Club Now!' button and follow the instructions.

What games do you play?

We specialize in FPS games such as Battlefield V, CS2, and Valorant. Join us for some intense gaming sessions!

Is Discord required to join?

While Discord is not mandatory, we highly recommend joining our Discord server for better communication and coordination during gameplay.

Can I suggest new games?

Absolutely! We are always open to suggestions for new games to explore and conquer together as a club.

How can I contact you?

For any inquiries or to reach out to us, feel free to join our Discord server using the link provided.
